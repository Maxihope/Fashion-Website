Our Story

Fashion Hub began her career in the fashion industry as an Image Consultant before swiftly transitioning into fashion design. She honed her technical skills in fashion through a combination of self-study and courses at the University of Arts London College of Fashion and Central Saint Martins. With a deep appreciation for luxury fashion, she soon identified a gap in the market for brands that specifically serve black and brown women, particularly those with unconventional body proportions like her own.

Our Business

At the core of our operations is a forward-thinking on-demand production model that enables us to deliver a seamless, direct-to-consumer experience while minimizing waste.
We are dedicated to building a more sustainable future for both our brand and the planet. Beyond our innovative production approach, we carefully choose our fabrics and
materials to ensure that each garment is durable and long-lasting.We also practice small-batch sourcing to maintain quality and prevent overproduction, while leftover fabrics
are creatively repurposed into functional lifestyle products. Thanks to our worldwide shipping, we've reached customers in over 30 countries, connecting with women from diverse backgrounds
and cultures. This global reach has allowed us to create a truly inclusive and international FASHION HUB community.
